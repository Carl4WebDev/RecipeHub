Chicken Adobo

    Ingredients:

  • 500g chicken (cut into pieces)
  • ½ cup soy sauce
  • ¼ cup vinegar
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 1 tsp peppercorn
  • 1 cup water

    Steps:

  1. In a pot, combine chicken, soy sauce, vinegar, garlic, bay leaf, and peppercorn.
  2. Let it marinate for 30 minutes (optional but recommended).
  3. Bring to a boil, then add water.
  4. Simmer on low heat for 30–40 minutes until chicken is tender.
  5. Pour ¼ cup batter and cook until bubbles form. Flip and cook until golden.
  6. Let the sauce reduce to desired thickness. Serve with rice.

Spaghetti Aglio

    Ingredients:

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 4 cloves garlic (sliced)
  • ¼ cup olive oil
  • 1 tsp chili flakes
  • Salt
  • Chopped parsley

    Steps:

  1. Cook spaghetti in salted boiling water. Reserve ½ cup pasta water, then drain.
  2. In a pan, heat olive oil over low heat.
  3. Add garlic and chili flakes. Cook until garlic is golden.
  4. Add drained spaghetti and pasta water.
  5. Toss everything together. Top with parsley and serve hot.

Pancakes

    Ingredients:

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 tbsp sugar
  • 1 tbsp baking powder
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 egg
  • 2 tbsp melted butter
  • Pinch of salt

    Steps:

  1. In a bowl, mix flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt.
  2. In another bowl, whisk milk, egg, and melted butter.
  3. Combine wet and dry ingredients; stir until just mixed (lumpy is okay).
  4. Heat a non-stick pan and lightly grease.
  5. Pour ¼ cup batter and cook until bubbles form. Flip and cook until golden.
  6. Serve with syrup, fruits, or butter.
